How To Make Money Muffins
  1. Mix flower, sugar, and baking powder in a small bowl
  2. Mix eggs, honey, oat milk, and vanilla in a separate larger bowl
  3. Add the dry mixture to the liquid one in twwo batches, mixed thoroughly
  4. Distribute mixture into greased muffin tins
  5. Bake for 16 minutes, or until lightly browned
  6. Enjoy!
